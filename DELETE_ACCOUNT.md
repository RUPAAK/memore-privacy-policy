# Deleting Your Memore Account

To request deletion of your Memore account and all associated data, please submit the following information via our Google Form: [https://forms.gle/YXmQtifUQhycqGP29]

### Data that will be deleted:
- Notes, to-do lists, bucket lists
- Shared images and albums
- Anniversary dates
- Account credentials (username, email)

We will process deletion requests within 7 days. Any data retained for legal or backup purposes will be anonymized and deleted within 30 days.
